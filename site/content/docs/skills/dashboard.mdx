## When to use

When you want a detailed breakdown of savings across tools, time periods, and projects — more than `/ashlr-savings` provides.

## What it does

Reads `~/.ashlr/stats.json` and renders:

1. Per-tool bar chart (ASCII) showing tokens saved by tool for the last 7 days
2. 30-day trend line
3. Top 5 projects by savings
4. Best single day
5. Calibration status (whether the savings multipliers have been calibrated for your model)
